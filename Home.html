DESIGNED FOR STUDENTS, EDUCATORS & COUNSELLORS

At CareerGuide, we believe future preparation and career guidance counselling are equally important. That’s why we’ve brought everything together in one fun, easily accessible platform.

The site is designed with parents, students, teachers and counsellors in mind, matching those starting their career journeys, with the experts best able to help them. Parents can track what their learners are achieving on the site, giving them peace of mind that their child is on the best path for a successful career, whilst educators and counsellors will have the best tools available to assist.

Through CareerGuide, the entire journey of a Learner is supported and encouraged, from the first step of self discovery to the last lap of being ushered into their dream career.

We put all of the information at a student’s fingertips, from what day-to-day life in a chosen career would be like, to the best colleges and universities to help them get there.

As well as this, the platform offers career counselling that provides personalised one-to-one career guidance for students.

And it's all in one place!
